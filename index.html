About

Sligo Tropics was established in 2022. It was founded by two college students in Sligo who's local team was moved to Donegal. With no local club in their hometown, they used this oppurtunity to set up their own club.

The club consists of 16 different teams ranging from ages as young as 5 and over. Their are two senior men and womens teams who play every second Saturday in the Knocknarae areana situated in the ATU campus.

We are deligheted to be sponsored by many local businesses around Sligo. If this is something of interest to you please get in contact with us through our contact page.
